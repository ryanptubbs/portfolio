Getting real curious now are we?

My name is Ryan Tubbs. I'm a digital artist and illustrator based in Colorado. I have a degree in journalism and media comms but working in that industry quickly burned me out and pushed me towards more creative, independent ways to make money.

My work aims to keep one foot in the world of the tactile and analog while embracing modern software and techniques in my design process. I love structured compositions, hand-drawn elements, bold lines and colors. Humor and empathy should underlie everything.
